What is a Ceaser Cipher?
Basic way to make secert message

Caesar Cipher is a basic way to encoded messages. An encoded message makes it harder for others to read your intended message.It is one of the oldest and simplest encryption techniques. A Ceaser cipher shifts a letters position by a number.

How to encode your own message
Power comes from shifting

The technique that Ceaser cipher deploys is know as a substitution cipher. A substitution cipher is a process of subsiting the letters in the text with something else. In a Ceaser cipher , the letters are shift by their posiition in the alphabet.

How to decode
Have the key handy

Decoding the message is a simple task if you have the key. You just go backwards by the amount you went foward. For example . a message with the letter A and a key of 2 would result in an encoded message of C. To get the orginal message you would have to go backwards 2 which would give you A.
